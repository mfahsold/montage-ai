# Montage AI - Positioning & Messaging Strategy

> **Single Source of Truth** for all marketing, documentation, and public communications.
> If it contradicts this document, it is wrong.

## Core Identity

*   **Product Name**: Montage AI
*   **Tagline**: "We do not generate pixels; we polish them."
*   **Short Description**: The local-first AI assistant that turns raw footage into beat-synced montages.
*   **License Status**: **Source Available** (PolyForm Noncommercial).
    *   *Do NOT use:* "Open Source" (without qualification), "MIT", "Apache".
    *   *Do use:* "Source Available", "Free for Personal Use", "Open for Contribution".

## Key Value Propositions (The "Why")

1.  **Polish, Don't Generate**: We are the antidote to AI hallucinations. We respect the source footage.
2.  **Privacy First**: Your footage stays on your drive. No mandatory cloud upload.
3.  **NLE Interoperability**: We are not a walled garden. We export `.otio` and `.edl` to DaVinci Resolve/Premiere.
4.  **Hybrid Power**: Runs locally on consumer hardware, with optional cloud offloading (cgpu) for heavy tasks.
