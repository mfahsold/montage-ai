# Roadmap 2026

**Strategic Focus:** Polish, Don't Generate.
**Q1 Theme:** Preview-First & Pro-Handoff.

---

## Q1 2026 (Jan - Mar)

### Month 1: Foundation & Preview-First
*   **Transcript Editor:** Live preview flow, word-level cut list, filler removal.
*   **Preview Pipeline:** Default 360p render on upload, clear progress UI.
*   **Telemetry:** Time-to-preview metrics.

### Month 2: Shorts Studio 2.0
*   **Smart Reframe v2:** Subject tracking + motion smoothing.
*   **Caption Styles:** TikTok/Bold/Karaoke presets with live preview.
*   **Highlights:** MVP detection & review cards.

### Month 3: Pro Polish & Handoff
*   **Pro Handoff Pack:** OTIO export, Proxy generation, Relink guides.
*   **Audio Polish:** Voice Isolation + Denoise toggle with SNR check.
*   **Performance:** Meeting "Time-to-First-Preview < 3 min" KPI.
